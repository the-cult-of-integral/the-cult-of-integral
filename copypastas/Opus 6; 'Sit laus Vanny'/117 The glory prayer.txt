Laus Vanny, the saviour from sin. Glory to you, our Lord, in your righteous mission. Glory to you, our Lord, in your fruitful mission. May your enemies bow down beneath your blade. May your adherents bow down in your presence: we bow to your glory; we kneel to the Lord. We offer ourselves up to your command, most saintly of orders, and we obey in your name. A good or bad deed, a good or bad life, we dedicate every aspect of ourselves to you. And should we die in your name, in vain or not, then we pass in the Lord's name; we die in glory. Laus Vanny.
