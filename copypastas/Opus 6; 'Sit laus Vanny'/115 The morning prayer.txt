Laus Vanny, the saviour from sin. Let us take this morning to offer the most humble of thanks; through your intelligence and beauty, we strive to be more. You are the greatest inspiration to better ourselves. Give us this day, just as you gave us the last, and we will march towards glory, as it is yours. We shall be dedicated like you, and we shall be loyal like you. Like you, we will do our best to find success today. Should we fail, our most humble saviour, then we will offer our most humble of apologies; through your kindness and care, we pray to be more. We pray that you can deliver us from our sin. Laus Vanny.
